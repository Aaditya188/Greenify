About Us

What our project actually does is asks the user to click a picture of your crop and then it scans the entire picture and compares it with the other pictures on the database and detects the faults in the crop and tells us if the crop is healthy or maybe suffering from disease.

  • With Keras-Module, our application can iterate at the speed of thought.
  • Bigger Database! Bigger accuracy.
  • Gives you full fledged analysis.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How accurate is our application?

    Initially our website is not very accurate because of less training of the ML model but when we add more datasets, the accuracy of the outcome will gradually increase.

  • How did we make our website / What tools did we use in the make of our website?

    We used HTML(for the Framework), CSS(for Exhibition of website), Java Script(for the backend of the entire website).

  • How can we scan and upload our images?

    When you open our website, you will see a scan option at the very front page and on clicking on that, you will be redirected to a page where you can upload your crop’s picture and get the results.

  • Where will the images be stored and processed?

    All the photos that are uploaded are stored on our flask database and also simultaneously stored into the datasets to improve the accuracy of the model.

  • What all features does the website has?

    Our website tells you about the details of your crop and ways you can make it better, current weather around you, it gives you a chatbot for assistance around the website and it also tells you about the team that worked on the project.

  • Will we get to know the specific disease from which the crop is suffering?

    No, not right now but it is scheduled in the further stages of development of our website.
